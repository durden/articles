# Lists

## Unordered list

- item 2

or

* item 2

## Ordered list

1. item 1
1. item 2

or

1. item 1
2. item 2

## Nesting

1. item 1
  1. item 1.1
  2. item 1.2
    - subitem 1
    - subitem 2
2. item 2

## Task Listing

- [ ] item 1
- [x] item 2
